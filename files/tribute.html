Professor Pat Utomi

  • 1956: He was born on February 6, 1956 in , Kaduna State. He is an indigene of Igbuzo in Oshimili North Local Government Area of Delta State.
  • 1962: Our Lady of Fatima, Gusau from 1962 to 1966; Christ the King College, Onitsha and Loyola College, Ibadan, finishing his secondary education at the age of 15.
  • 1977: Due to the fact that he was not up to the entry age for university, which was 17, he enrolled at the Federal School of Arts and Science.He therafter got admission into the University of Nigeria,Nsukka in 1973 to study Mass Communication and graduated in 1977.
  • 2003: He then proceeded to Indiana University, Bloomington, Indiana, USA, where he got his Ph.D. MPA, MA. He was Appointed professor of the Social and Political Economy Environment of Business and Pioneer Entrepreneurship Teacher at Lagos Business School in 2003
  • 2006: He has also written commissioned papers for the UKs DFID including the latest collaboration with Colleagues from Oxford on the Political Economy of growth in Nigeria. (2006).
